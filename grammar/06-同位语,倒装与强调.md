### 倒装的本质就是强调 (任何一个成分都可以被强调)
- 非正常语序
- 前置


----

#### `主语`强调
##### eg1: He beat me!
- `It was` he `that` beat me!

#### `宾语`强调

##### eg2: He beat me!
- `It was` me `that` he beat

#### `状语`强调

##### eg3: He beat me yeasterday.
- `It was` yesterday `that` he beat me

#### `谓语强调`

##### eg3: He beat me yeasterday.
