### 强调句结构

- It is/was + (被强调成分)  + that/who + 句子剩余成分

----

#### `主语`强调
##### eg1: He beat me!
- `It was` he `that` beat me!

---

#### `宾语`强调

##### eg2: He beat me!
- `It was` me `that` he beat

---
#### `状语`强调

##### eg3: He beat me yesterday.
- `It was` yesterday `that` he beat me

---

#### `谓语强调`

##### eg4: He beat me yesterday.

- He `did` beat me yesterday
